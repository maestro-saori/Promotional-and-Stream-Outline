Promotional Plan

Learn better. Study smarter. Build excitement for your educational livestream.

Back to Home Stream Outline

Day 1 – Announcement

  • Main poster + livestream details
  • Include title, date, time, platform
  • Short teaser description
  • CTA: “Save the date!”
  • Ask classmates to share

Day 2 – Teaser Video

  • 10–30 second teaser clip
  • Tip preview or quick hack
  • Text overlay: “Want more study tips? Watch the livestream!”
  • CTA: “Follow so you won’t miss it!”

Day 3 – Value Post

  • Share 1–2 educational tips
  • Shows what viewers can expect
  • CTA: “More tips on the livestream!”

Day 4 – Engagement Post

  • Ask a question for interaction
  • Examples:
    • “What’s your biggest study struggle?”
    • “Focus or motivation?”
    • “Do you struggle with independent learning?”
  • CTA: Invite them to the livestream

Day 5 – Countdown (2 days left)

  • Post: Countdown poster
  • Caption: “2 days left before our Educational Tips Livestream!”
  • Extra: Add reminder stickers (IG/Fb Stories)

Day 6 – Final Reminder (1 day left)

  • Post: Short clip or poster
  • Caption: “Watch our livestream tomorrow, at (time)!”
  • CTA: “Turn on notifications.”

Day 7 – Livestream Day

  • Post: Morning reminder
  • Before going live: Post in stories “Going Live later at (time) don’t forget!!!”
